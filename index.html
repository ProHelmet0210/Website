Why riders use ProHelmet

Helmets are used daily and can trap sweat and odour over time. ProHelmet helps maintain a fresher feel and more comfortable daily use.

Freshness

  • Helps reduce unpleasant odour buildup
  • Helps keep the interior feeling fresher
  • Encourages consistent helmet hygiene

Comfort

  • Supports cleaner, more comfortable wear
  • Useful for frequent commuters
  • Ideal after long rides or hot days

Convenience

  • Self-service, guided experience
  • Fast cycle built for public footfall
  • No staff required

How to use the kiosk

Simple steps. Clear progress. Predictable completion.

1) Place the helmet

Insert your helmet and close the chamber. Follow the on-screen prompts.

2) Start the cycle

Complete payment (UPI/card). The system begins the sanitizing sequence automatically.

3) Collect and go

When the cycle completes, remove your helmet and continue your ride.

  • Note If the kiosk indicates a problem or your cycle doesn’t start after payment, contact support with your transaction ID.

Safety & usage guidance

Designed for public use. Please follow these basics for best results.

Use as intended

  • Do not force the door or tamper with the kiosk
  • Remove loose items (clips/straps) that may obstruct the chamber
  • Follow on-screen instructions during the cycle

What to expect

  • Guided progress display during sanitizing
  • Controlled automated stages
  • Clear completion prompt at the end

Limitations

  • Not intended to repair helmets or remove physical damage
  • May not remove permanent stains
  • For best upkeep, wipe the helmet exterior regularly

FAQ

Quick answers for common questions.

How often should I sanitize?

For most riders, once every 1–2 weeks helps maintain freshness and comfort, depending on usage and weather.

What if I paid but the cycle didn’t start?

Message us on WhatsApp with your transaction ID and screenshot. We will verify and help promptly.

Is payment secure?

Payments are processed through Razorpay. We do not store your card or bank details.
