Why ProHelmet

Helmets are worn daily, but rarely cleaned properly. Over time they can trap sweat and odour — and may contribute to discomfort and irritation. ProHelmet makes helmet hygiene effortless in public spaces.

How It Works

A simple, predictable process — designed for trust and repeat usage.

1) Place helmet

Insert the helmet and close the chamber. The kiosk detects placement and starts the guided flow.

2) Sanitizing cycle

Automated stages (example: UVC → fog → air dry) run in a controlled sequence.

3) Collect & go

When complete, remove the helmet and continue your ride with confidence.

  • Professional user experience designed to avoid confusion and build confidence.
  • Operator dashboard + reporting designed for business visibility.
